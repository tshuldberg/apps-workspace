## 1. Solar Warden

**Evidence Rating: 2/10** (Single unverified witness, no corroborating documents released)

### Core Claims

Solar Warden is an alleged U.S. Navy-operated space fleet consisting of spacecraft capable of interstellar travel, reportedly operating under the Naval Network and Space Operations Command (NNSOC). The program's existence was brought to public attention through the hacking activities of Gary McKinnon, a Scottish systems administrator.

### The Gary McKinnon Hack (2001-2002)

Between March 2001 and March 2002, Gary McKinnon accessed 97 U.S. military and NASA computers in what U.S. authorities called "the biggest military computer hack of all time." His stated motivation was finding evidence of UFOs, antigravity technology, and suppressed "free energy."

**What McKinnon claims to have found:**

- An Excel spreadsheet titled "Non-Terrestrial Officers" containing ranks and names of individuals not matching any known military unit
- A separate tab listing "fleet-to-fleet transfers" between ships
- Ship names with "USSS" (United States Space Ship) designations, including the alleged **USSS LeMay** and **USSS Hillenkoetter** (named after General Curtis LeMay and CIA Director Roscoe Hillenkoetter)
- Approximately 8-10 ship names that did not appear in any public Navy registry
- Approximately 300 personnel listed in the program
- At NASA's Johnson Space Center Building 8, a folder titled "Unfiltered" containing raw satellite imagery showing cigar-shaped objects with domes, which he claimed were being systematically removed from publicly released images

**Key Details:**

- McKinnon used a Perl script to search for blank administrator passwords on military networks, finding many unsecured systems
- He accessed systems belonging to the U.S. Army, Navy, Air Force, Department of Defense, and NASA
- He caused an estimated $900,000 in damages, including shutting down a Washington D.C. network serving 2,000 users for three days
- He was diagnosed with Asperger syndrome during the legal proceedings

### Legal Outcome

McKinnon faced extradition to the United States where he could have received up to 70 years in prison. After a decade-long legal battle, UK Home Secretary Theresa May blocked extradition on October 16, 2012, citing human rights concerns -- specifically the risk of suicide given his mental health condition. British prosecutors also declined to pursue charges in the UK.

### Evidence Assessment

McKinnon never saved screenshots or copies of the data he claims to have seen. His account rests entirely on his verbal testimony. The ship names he mentioned (LeMay, Hillenkoetter) are suspiciously convenient -- both are figures already connected to UFO lore (Hillenkoetter was a founding member of NICAP, a civilian UFO organization). No independent verification of the "Non-Terrestrial Officers" spreadsheet has ever surfaced.

## 2. Dark Fleet

**Evidence Rating: 1/10** (Entirely based on unverifiable whistleblower testimony)

### Core Claims

The "Dark Fleet" (German: *Nachtwaffen*) is an alleged breakaway German/extraterrestrial military space fleet that supposedly originated from Nazi Antarctic bases during World War II. The concept is primarily associated with the research of Michael Salla, a former American University professor who pivoted to "exopolitics."

### Narrative Framework

According to Salla's work, the Dark Fleet narrative follows this timeline:

1. **1930s-1940s:** Nazi Germany made contact with an extraterrestrial group called the "Draco Reptilians"
2. A secret agreement was reached, providing the Nazis with advanced technology
3. A breakaway German colony was established in Antarctica's extensive underground cavern systems
4. Using reverse-engineered alien spacecraft, this colony built a fleet of advanced vessels
5. After WWII, the Dark Fleet expanded to bases on the Moon, Mars, and throughout the solar system
6. The fleet allegedly participates in a "galactic slave trade" involving millions of missing people

### Key Sources and Researchers

- **Michael Salla:** Published *Antarctica's Hidden History: Corporate Foundations of Secret Space Programs* (2018) and *Galactic Federations, Councils & Secret Space Programs* (2022). Salla's research draws primarily on testimony from Corey Goode, William Tompkins, Vladimir Terziski, and "Dr. Pete Peterson"
- **Len Kasten:** Published *Dark Fleet* through Simon & Schuster's Inner Traditions imprint
- **Vladimir Terziski:** Bulgarian-born researcher who presented alleged evidence of Nazi Antarctic bases at UFO conferences in the 1990s

### Connection to Nazi Antarctica (Neuschwabenland)

The historical kernel is that Nazi Germany's 1938-1939 expedition to Queen Maud Land (which they named Neuschwabenland) did occur. However, mainstream historians document this as a whaling and territorial claim operation, not a secret base construction project. The post-war Operation Highjump (1946-1947), led by Admiral Byrd, is cited by proponents as evidence of military confrontation with Antarctic Nazis, though officially it was a training exercise and territorial survey.

### Evidence Assessment

The Dark Fleet concept rests on a chain of unverifiable claims from individuals whose credibility is itself in question (see Section 5 on SSP Whistleblowers). There are no documents, photographs, or physical evidence. The narrative combines real historical events (Nazi Antarctic expedition, Operation Highjump) with increasingly fantastical claims.

## 3. TR-3B / Aurora

**Evidence Rating: 3/10** (Real sightings of triangular craft exist; specific TR-3B claims are unverified)

### Core Claims

The TR-3B (sometimes called "Black Manta" or "Astra") is an alleged classified reconnaissance aircraft or spacecraft using antigravity propulsion technology. The "B" designation distinguishes it from the conventional TR-3A "Black Manta," signaling a fundamentally different craft. It is described as a triangular vehicle capable of hovering silently, emitting a central glow, and departing with extreme acceleration.

### Alleged Technical Specifications

- Triangular airframe with rounded corners
- Central rotating ring filled with mercury-based plasma (called the "Magnetic Field Disrupter" or MFD)
- The MFD allegedly reduces the craft's mass by 89%, allowing for extreme maneuverability
- Three rocket engines at each corner for conventional propulsion
- Capable of Mach 9+ speeds vertically and horizontally
- Nearly silent operation

### Key Triangle Craft Sightings

**Belgian UFO Wave (1989-1990):**
- Between November 1989 and April 1990, approximately 2,000 sightings of triangular craft were reported across Belgium
- On March 30, 1990, one object was tracked on radar and two Belgian Air Force F-16s were scrambled to investigate
- Neither pilot visually confirmed the object; subsequent analysis attributed some radar contacts to atmospheric interference (Bragg scattering)
- This remains one of the most extensively documented mass UFO sighting events

**Phoenix Lights (March 13, 1997):**
- Thousands of witnesses across Arizona reported a massive V-shaped or triangular craft moving slowly and silently
- Witnesses included physicians, pilots, and Arizona Governor Fife Symington, who initially ridiculed reports but later confirmed he also saw the object
- The U.S. military attributed the lights to A-10 Warthog flare drops from the Barry Goldwater Range, an explanation many witnesses reject

### Connection to Salvatore Pais Patents

The U.S. Navy has filed patents (see Section 16) for technologies that would theoretically enable TR-3B-type craft, including inertial mass reduction and high-energy electromagnetic field generation. However, no evidence connects these patents to an operational vehicle.

### John Lear Claims

John Lear, son of Learjet inventor Bill Lear and a former CIA pilot, made extensive claims about secret antigravity craft and government contact with extraterrestrials before his death in 2022. His claims were influential in shaping the TR-3B mythology but remain entirely unverified.

### Evidence Assessment

Black triangle sightings are among the most commonly reported UFO types worldwide, and the Belgian wave and Phoenix Lights represent genuinely unexplained mass sighting events. However, the specific "TR-3B" designation and technical details (mercury plasma drive, mass reduction) originate from anonymous sources and have never been confirmed by any verifiable documentation or defector.

## 4. MILAB (Military Abduction Program)

**Evidence Rating: 2/10** (Academic framework exists from Lammer, but claims rest on recovered memories)

### Core Claims

MILAB (Military Abduction) refers to the theory that some alien abduction experiences are actually covert military operations involving kidnapping, drugging, and mind-control experimentation on civilians. The phenomenon was first systematically investigated by Dr. Helmut Lammer of the Austrian Space Research Institute.

### Helmut Lammer's Research

Lammer, who held a Ph.D. and worked at a legitimate aerospace research institution, published *MILABS: Military Mind Control and Alien Abduction* (1999), which was the first book to document evidence connecting covert military mind control with the alien abduction phenomenon.

**Key findings from Lammer's research:**

- Some abduction experiencers reported being taken not by aliens but by human military personnel
- Experiencers described being drugged, transported to hospital-like rooms and underground military facilities, examined by men in white lab coats, and sometimes implanted with devices
- He documented parallels between MILAB accounts and known CIA programs ARTICHOKE and MK-ULTRA
- He distinguished between purely ET abduction claims and hybrid military/ET scenarios

### Supersoldier Program Claims

A subset of MILAB claimants describe being recruited into "supersoldier" programs involving:
- Genetic enhancement
- Combat training under altered states of consciousness
- Memory wiping and reinsertion into normal life
- Physical augmentation through classified technology

These claims overlap significantly with "20 & Back" narratives (see Section 7) and are primarily promoted through alternative media and conferences rather than academic channels.

### Difference from ET Abductions

According to the MILAB framework:
- **ET abductions:** Involve non-human entities, telepathic communication, biological sampling, often in isolated locations
- **MILABs:** Involve human military personnel, drugging, transport by helicopter or van to military-style facilities, interrogation about ET contact experiences, implantation with tracking devices

### Evidence Assessment

Lammer's academic credentials lend some structural credibility to the framework, and the historical reality of MK-ULTRA and ARTICHOKE programs (confirmed by declassified CIA documents) demonstrates that military/intelligence agencies did conduct covert experiments on unwitting civilians. However, MILAB claims themselves rest almost entirely on recovered memories, hypnotic regression, and anecdotal reports -- none of which have been independently verified.

## 6. Interplanetary Corporate Conglomerate (ICC)

**Evidence Rating: 1/10** (Entirely based on SSP whistleblower testimony)

### Core Claims

The ICC is described as a consortium of defense contractors and other corporations that have pooled resources to create a vast infrastructure in the solar system. According to SSP narratives:

- Corporations including Lockheed Martin, Northrop Grumman, and Boeing (plus non-aerospace companies) formed the ICC
- Governed by "Super Boards" or "Super Committees"
- Operates 100+ colonies, bases, and industrial facilities on the Moon, Mars, the asteroid belt, and other solar system bodies
- Specializes in trade with non-human, off-world groups
- Operates independently from any national government
- Uses slave labor (connecting to MILAB and "20 & Back" narratives)

### Real-World Parallels

While the ICC as described has no evidence, the conspiracy theory draws on real structural elements:
- The military-industrial complex is well documented, with revolving doors between defense contractors and the Pentagon
- Lockheed Martin's Skunk Works, Northrop Grumman's classified programs, and Boeing's defense division do operate on classified contracts
- The DOD's black budget (see Section 8) funds programs that are genuinely hidden from public oversight
- Special Access Programs (SAPs) and Unacknowledged SAPs (USAPs) create real compartmentalization

### Evidence Assessment

The ICC concept is a narrative framework built entirely on SSP whistleblower claims (primarily Corey Goode). No documents, contracts, financial records, or physical evidence have been presented. It represents the conspiracy theory's attempt to explain how such a massive secret program could be funded and operated.

## 7. 20 & Back Program

**Evidence Rating: 1/10** (Unfalsifiable by design; no physical evidence)

### Core Claims

The "20 and Back" is an alleged protocol used by secret space programs whereby individuals serve 20 years off-planet, then undergo:

1. **Age regression:** Physical de-aging by 20 years using pharmaceutical immobilization and "molecular regression" technology over a two-week period
2. **Memory wipe:** Erasure of all memories from the 20-year service period, with "screen memories" implanted in their place
3. **Timeline reinsertion:** Time travel back to the moment of original recruitment, so no time has passed in "normal" life
4. **Memory recovery:** After 20 years of normal life have elapsed, memories of the SSP service allegedly begin to resurface

### First Known Claimant

The concept first appeared publicly in 2000 with Michael Relfe's *The Mars Records*, in which he describes serving on Mars from 1976 to 1996, followed by age regression and memory wiping. His wife Stephanie Relfe published his hypnotic regression sessions.

### Known Claimants

Multiple individuals have come forward claiming "20 and Back" experiences, including:
- Michael Relfe (first public account, 2000)
- Corey Goode (most famous claimant, public from ~2014)
- Randy Cramer (Mars Defense Force)
- Tony Rodrigues (Ceres slave labor)
- Jean Charles Moyen (French-Canadian filmmaker, more recent claimant)

### The Unfalsifiability Problem

The "20 and Back" concept is designed to be inherently unfalsifiable:
- Memories are wiped, so lack of memory is explained
- Time travel means no one was "missing"
- Physical age regression means there is no physical evidence
- Memories that do "return" conveniently cannot be verified
- Anyone who claims to have no such experience simply hasn't "recovered" their memories yet

### Evidence Assessment

No physical evidence, documentation, or independent verification exists for any "20 and Back" claim. The framework is a self-reinforcing narrative that explains away the absence of evidence as a feature of the program's design.

## 8. Missing Pentagon Money

**Evidence Rating: 7/10** (Genuine accounting anomalies documented by academic researchers and government auditors; interpretation varies)

### Core Facts

This is one of the few topics in the SSP domain with substantial, verifiable evidence:

**The $21 Trillion Finding:**
- In 2017, economist Dr. Mark Skidmore of Michigan State University, working with Catherine Austin Fitts, documented $21 trillion in "unsupported journal voucher adjustments" in Department of Defense and HUD accounts between 1998 and 2015
- These were identified using the DOD Office of Inspector General's own published reports
- The $6.5 trillion figure for Army adjustments in fiscal year 2015 alone was documented by the DOD OIG
- The research was [published by MSU](https://msutoday.msu.edu/news/2017/12/msu-scholars-find-21-trillion-in-unauthorized-government-spending-defense-department-to-conduct)

**Important Caveat:**
- "Unsupported adjustments" are not the same as "missing money" -- they represent accounting entries that cannot be traced to their source
- These are journal voucher transactions that can be counted multiple times as they pass through accounts
- Skidmore and Fitts themselves do not claim all $21 trillion was secret or misused
- The adjustments appear on both positive and negative sides of the ledger, potentially netting each other out

### Catherine Austin Fitts

- Former Assistant Secretary of Housing and Urban Development under George H.W. Bush
- Founded the Solari Report, which tracks government financial accountability
- Maintains the [Missing Money](https://missingmoney.solari.com/) website with supporting documentation
- Her analysis connects black budget funding to potential secret programs
- She is a legitimate former government official with real financial expertise, though some of her conclusions venture into speculative territory

### Pentagon Audit History

- The DOD was the last major federal agency to undergo an audit
- Its first-ever audit began in 2018 (announced shortly after the $21 trillion research was published)
- The Pentagon has **failed every audit** since then
- After announcing the audit, the government adopted new rules allowing officials to "misstate and move funds around to hide expenditures if deemed necessary for national security purposes"

### The Known Black Budget

The classified budget is real and documented:
- **FY2025:** DOD requested $89.9 billion in classified program funding (10.6% of total DOD discretionary budget)
- **FY2019:** $81.1 billion total black budget ($59.9B National Intelligence Program + $21.2B Military Intelligence Program)
- **FY2015:** Approximately $58.7 billion in classified programs
- Most classified funding is routed through the Department of the Air Force budget ($45.1B in "pass-through funding" for FY2025)

### Connection to SSP Claims

SSP proponents argue that the unaccounted funds represent financing for secret space programs, reverse-engineering projects, and off-planet infrastructure. While the accounting anomalies are real, the leap to space program funding is speculative. The money could fund any number of classified programs, or could simply represent systemic accounting dysfunction in an organization that never developed proper financial controls.

## 13. Ceres Bases

**Evidence Rating: 1/10** (NASA data explained scientifically; SSP claims are unverifiable add-ons)

### Dawn Mission Imagery

NASA's Dawn spacecraft orbited Ceres from 2015 to 2018, capturing detailed imagery that revealed:

- **Bright spots in Occator Crater:** The most prominent anomaly -- bright spots (faculae) within an 80 km (50 mi) crater
- Initial mystery generated significant public interest and speculation
- **NASA's explanation (2020):** The bright areas are deposits of sodium carbonate from a deep subsurface brine reservoir. Salt-enriched water percolated to the surface and evaporated, leaving highly reflective salt crusts
- Some deposits are less than 2 million years old, and geological activity may be ongoing
- Additional bright spots found elsewhere on Ceres, all consistent with the brine/salt model

- He describes a German-led Dark Fleet base on Ceres
- The colony allegedly serves as a logistics and supply hub for deeper space operations

### Evidence Assessment

The Dawn mission's scientific findings comprehensively explain the bright spots as natural geological phenomena. There is no anomalous data in Dawn's imagery that requires an artificial explanation. The SSP claims about Ceres bases rest entirely on unverifiable testimony layered onto public interest in the genuinely mysterious (at the time) bright spots.

## 14. USAP (Unacknowledged Special Access Projects)

**Evidence Rating: 5/10** (USAPs are real classification categories; specific claims about their contents are speculative)

### The Classification System

USAPs are real and documented within the U.S. classification system:

- **Special Access Programs (SAPs):** Programs requiring access authorization beyond standard Top Secret clearance
- **Acknowledged SAPs:** Their existence can be confirmed, though details remain classified
- **Unacknowledged SAPs (USAPs):** Their very existence is classified -- if asked, authorized personnel must deny the program exists
- **Waived SAPs:** The most restricted category, exempt from normal congressional reporting requirements

This framework means programs can exist that even the President, senior military officials, and congressional oversight committees may not know about.

### Steven Greer's Disclosure Project

**The 2001 National Press Club Event:**
- On May 9, 2001, Dr. Steven Greer organized a press conference featuring 20+ retired military, intelligence, FAA, and corporate witnesses

**Notable witness categories:**
- Military radar operators who tracked anomalous objects
- Air Force personnel who witnessed UFOs at nuclear weapons sites

- Corporate contractors who describe reverse-engineering work

**The 2017 Documentary:**
Greer released *Unacknowledged*, a crowdfunded documentary directed by Michael Mazzola and narrated by Giancarlo Esposito, expanding on the 2001 testimony.

### Greer's Credibility

Greer occupies a complex position:
- He has legitimate credentials (former emergency room physician)

  - CE-5 (Close Encounters of the Fifth Kind) contact protocols that critics call pseudoscientific

The existence of USAPs is not a conspiracy theory -- it is documented government classification policy. The question is what USAPs contain. The 2001 witness testimony is the strongest public evidence for potential UFO-related USAPs, with multiple military witnesses with verifiable backgrounds. However, testimony alone, without corroborating documentation, remains circumstantial.

## 18. Breakaway Civilization Concept

### Richard Dolan's Framework

Richard Dolan, a historian and one of the most academically rigorous UFO researchers, coined the term "breakaway civilization" to describe:

> A radically advanced and increasingly separate structure that has access to classified science and data denied to the rest of us.

### Core Thesis

Dolan's argument proceeds logically:

1. **The secrecy is real:** Trillions in unaccounted defense spending, USAPs, compartmentalized programs
2. **Technology divergence:** If secret programs have been developing advanced technology for 75+ years (since WWII), the gap between classified and public technology could be enormous
3. **Self-sustaining secrecy:** At some point, the classified world becomes so advanced and so separated from the public world that it effectively constitutes a separate civilization
4. **Financial independence:** Through black budgets, corporate partnerships, and potentially other revenue sources, this structure could become financially self-sustaining
5. **Cultural separation:** Personnel in these programs may develop their own culture, priorities, and worldview distinct from the civilian population

### Intellectual Lineage

The breakaway civilization concept draws on:
- **Dwight Eisenhower's 1961 warning** about the military-industrial complex
- **James Bamford's work** on the NSA (*The Puzzle Palace*, *Body of Secrets*)
- **Tim Weiner's** *Legacy of Ashes: The History of the CIA*
- **Peter Dale Scott's** concept of the "deep state" (academic political science framework)
- The documented history of MKULTRA, COINTELPRO, and other once-secret programs that operated for decades before disclosure

### Dolan's Publications

- *UFOs and the National Security State, Volume 1: Chronology of a Cover-Up, 1941-1973* (2002)
- *UFOs and the National Security State, Volume 2: The Cover-Up Exposed, 1973-1991* (2009)

### The Most Obscure Extension

Some researchers extend the breakaway civilization concept to include:
- Multiple competing breakaway factions (military, corporate, ET-allied)
- Breakaway civilizations dating back thousands of years (connecting to ancient civilization theories)
- Temporal breakaway civilizations (using time travel to exist outside normal timeline)

### Evidence Assessment

Dolan's framework is the most intellectually respectable entry point into SSP theories because it builds from documented facts (classification systems, black budgets, historical government secrecy) to a logical extrapolation. The weakness is that the extrapolation, while coherent, remains speculative. The gap between "classified programs exist" and "a separate civilization has formed" requires evidence of the latter's existence, which has not been produced. However, as Dolan himself notes, a successfully hidden civilization would by definition be difficult to prove.
